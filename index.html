Security
Be safe online. Do not give out personal details (name, address, date of birth, etc) about yourself or others. Do not post your passwords or other sensitive information. I certainly don't need them. Any information you post could (probably) be browsed by spammers, scammers, identity stealers etc etc. You don't even need to register to post on the boards, so I won't take your passwords or email addresses. If you want, feel free to use an anonymising proxy to browse and post, or take someone else's name for a post or two (but I ask you, why?). Remember, you are not required to post, and your contribution may or may not be wanted, needed, or respected... but it's unlikely that it will be removed (not impossible).
Please try to use good (or even better, great) spelling and grammar as posts cannot be edited. You'll look like an idiot otherwise.
